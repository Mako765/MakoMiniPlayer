# Privacy Policy for MakoMiniPlayer

Last updated: May 2025

MakoMiniPlayer is a free, open-source Android TV video player.

## Data Collection
MakoMiniPlayer does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Third Party Services
- OpenSubtitles.com — used only when user manually searches for subtitles
- SubDL — used only when user manually searches for subtitles
- PayPal — donation link, opened in external browser

## Permissions
The app requests only permissions necessary for video playback and local file access.
